The Host

Pull up
a chair.

Glad You're Here is built around one simple idea: everyone has a story worth hearing. Each week, your host sits down with someone real — no scripts, no filters — and just talks.

About life, hard lessons, faith, and what it means to keep going. Think of it like the best conversation you've ever had at a coffee shop — the kind that stays with you long after the cup's gone cold.

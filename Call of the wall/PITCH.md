# Call of the Wall — Web Presence Proposal

## What I Built
A fully responsive, professional website for Call of the Wall that captures the spirit of the Friday 7am breakwall jump tradition. The site is designed to grow the community while keeping the event authentic and accessible.

**Live at:** `/index.html` (ready to deploy to any hosting)

---

## What's Included

### Core Features
- **Hero landing** — Compelling call-to-action with event details (time, location, what to bring)
- **Event info sections** — "The Jump," "How It Works," "The Rules," gallery space for photos
- **Responsive design** — Works perfectly on phone, tablet, and desktop
- **Social integration** — Direct links to @callof_thewall Instagram; built-in social sharing
- **Mobile-friendly CTA** — Easy-to-find "Get Directions" button for Friday morning attendance
- **SEO-ready** — Meta tags, structured data, and clean HTML for Google visibility
- **Professional polish** — Custom brand colors (red, blue, cream), smooth animations, typography hierarchy

### What's Ready to Add (Low effort)
- **Photo gallery** — Swap placeholder boxes with real event photos
- **Member sign-up form** — Capture emails for Friday reminders (via Formspree or similar)
- **Event counter** — "52 weeks a year, ∞ everyone welcome" — can track live attendance
- **Admin dashboard** (optional) — Manage gallery, track signups, event updates
- **Merchandise store link** — Link to merch or sponsorship opportunities
- **Analytics** — Google Analytics integration to track visitors and conversion

---

## Why This Matters

**Today:** The Friday tradition exists, but there's no central hub. New people don't know when/where to show up. Growth is word-of-mouth only.

**With this site:**
- Anyone can find you instantly (Google, social referral)
- New jumpers feel welcomed before they arrive (read the rules, see the vibe)
- You can measure growth and engagement (analytics, email list)
- It legitimizes the event — looks organized and intentional, not accidental
- **No selling out** — it stays free, grassroots, and authentic. Site just amplifies what already works.
